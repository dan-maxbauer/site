Hello! I created this blog as a place for me to learn new things and write about them. For my day job, I’m a visiting professor in geology at Carleton College. I teach about climate, paleobiology, and introductory geology. Its great and I love it. For my academic work, I regularly use R and have since I was a master’s student (~5 years now). I have wanted to be fancy and start a blog using Blogdown for a while, so, here it is. Now I’m fancy!

The theme of each post is likely to vary quite a bit here - and most content will likely be for me to learn about topics/tools I’m interested in more so than building a site that conveys expertise on a single topic.

Many posts will focus on my work in R - building different types of models/visualizations and exploring new data. But I’m also working towards learning Python, and I will incorportate some posts here and there that show some baby-steps towards that goal.

So, enjoy the ramblings! If there is anything you see in my posts that excites you (or horrifies you) feel free to reach out and get in touch.
